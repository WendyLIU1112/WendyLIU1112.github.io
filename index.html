Bio

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Singapore Management University (SMU), working with Prof. Denis Leung and Prof. Tomoki Fujii. Prior to this, I received my Master’s degree in Statistics under the supervision of Prof. Zhou Yu in 2021, and my Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics in 2018, both at East China Normal University.
