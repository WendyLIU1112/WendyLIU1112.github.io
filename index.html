Bio

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the School of Economics at Singapore Management University (SMU), where I work with Prof. Denis Leung and Prof. Tomoki Fujii. I received my Ph.D. in Statistics from East China Normal University in 2025 under the supervision of Prof. Yukun Liu. From 2024 to 2025, I was a visiting student at the University of California, Riverside, under the supervision of Prof. Shujie Ma. I also earned my Master’s degree in Statistics from East China Normal University in 2021 and my Bachelor’s degree in Statistics from Zhengzhou University in 2018.

What I Work On

Research Interests

My research develops rigorous statistical methods for causal questions arising in biomedical and applied research.

  • Causal Inference
  • Applied Biostatistics
  • Semiparametric Methods
  • Instrumental Variables
